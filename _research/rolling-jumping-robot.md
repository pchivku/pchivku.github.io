Inspired by Littlewood’s hopping hoop, we designed a robot that rolls and jumps from flat ground by driving an internal pendulum at high speed. It achieves vertical jumps of 2.4 body lengths.

<video controls autoplay muted loop playsinline width="800"><source src="/files/vertonly.mp4" type="video/mp4"></video>

By building rolling speed before takeoff, it can clear more than six body lengths horizontally and perform multiple jumps with little recovery time.

**Paper:** J. Buzhardt, P. Chivkula, and P. Tallapragada, “A Pendulum-Driven Legless Rolling Jumping Robot,” *IROS*, 2023. [Conference article](https://doi.org/10.1109/IROS55552.2023.10342513)
